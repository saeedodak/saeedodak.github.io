Saeed Odak

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Computational Geometry group of Professor Sándor Kisfaludi-Bak at Aalto University in Espoo, Finland.

I completed my PhD in Graphs and Geometry at the Algorithms, Graphs, and Geometry Lab at the University of Ottawa, under the supervision of Prosenjit Bose, Pat Morin, Michiel Smid, Anil Maheshwari, Jean-Lou De Carufel, and Vida Dujmović.

My research interests include algorithms, graph theory, and geometry.
